About Bridge of Care

Founded in 2023, Bridge of Care is a platform dedicated to connecting donors with verified NGOs and orphanages in need. Our mission is to create a seamless giving experience while ensuring transparency in the donation process.

We work with carefully vetted organizations across India to ensure your contributions reach those who need them most.
